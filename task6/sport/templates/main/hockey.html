Hockey

Fast, physical, and relentless, hockey turns every shift into a battle for space.

Hockey is played on ice, where players skate at high speed, move the puck quickly, and react in seconds. The pace of the game creates constant pressure and dramatic momentum changes.

Why it stands out

Few sports feel as intense as hockey. The action can switch from defense to attack almost instantly, and every line change can shift the rhythm of the game.

Key facts

  • Teams try to score by shooting the puck into the net.
  • The game is known for speed, contact, and quick transitions.
  • Sharp reactions and teamwork are essential on both ends of the ice.
