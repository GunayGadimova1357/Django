Football

The world game built on movement, patience, and sudden moments of brilliance.

Football is the most widely followed sport on the planet. Two teams of eleven players compete to control space, create chances, and score by putting the ball into the opponent's net.

Why it stands out

Football mixes structure and creativity. A match can feel tactical for long stretches, then change completely because of one pass, one run, or one finish.

Key facts

  • Each team has 11 players on the field.
  • A standard match has two 45-minute halves.
  • The main objective is to score more goals than the opponent.
